I had the chance to speak at [Web Day 2026](https://www.webdayconf.it/), the 9th edition of the conference organized by Improove in Milan, held on <time datetime="2026-03-30">March 30th, 2026</time> at Talent Garden Calabiana.

My talk was "Enhancing Web Responsiveness — A practical guide to debugging and avoiding slow interactions", and it was all about Interaction to Next Paint (INP), the Core Web Vital metric that measures how responsive a page is to user interactions.

I shared real-world case studies showing how to identify and fix poor INP, and practical techniques you can apply right away to make your websites feel snappier. If you've ever wondered why a button click feels sluggish or a form takes forever to respond, this one was for you.
