I recently had the pleasure of co-presenting with [Giulia Naponiello](https://www.linkedin.com/in/giulia-naponiello/), Senior Software Engineer at Red Hat, about gender bias in technology at two local meetups. The talk, titled "Rifattorizziamo i bias di genere, un commit alla volta" (Refactoring Gender Bias, One Commit at a Time), explored how unconscious biases subtly manifest in tech workplaces.

We shared personal stories and examined how bias shows up in everyday situations—from code review language to hiring assessments, and from the recognition of technical authority to team dynamics. The goal wasn't to point fingers, but to shine a light on the "cultural bugs" we all carry and suggest ways to fix them. Think of it as refactoring: same codebase, better outcomes. 🐛➡️✅
